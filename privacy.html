Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we protect and manage your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Email addresses for account creation
  • Music platform authentication tokens
  • Playlist data during transfers
  • Usage statistics and app performance data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Facilitate playlist transfers between music platforms
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Provide customer support
  • Send important updates about our service

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. Your data is stored securely and accessed only when necessary for providing our services.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with various music streaming platforms. When you use our service, you're also subject to their respective privacy policies.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at playlist@spatialreminders.ca.
